3. **Notifications**: Email only, no push notifications yet
4. **Analytics**: Limited reporting capabilities
5. **Real-time Features**: No live collaboration features
6. **Mobile App**: Web-only, no native mobile app
7. **Advanced Search**: Basic search, can be enhanced
8. **Bulk Operations**: Limited bulk editing capabilities
